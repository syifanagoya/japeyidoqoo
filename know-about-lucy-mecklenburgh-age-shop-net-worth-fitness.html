Lucy Mecklenburgh might be well known for her television career, she is also an accomplished entrepreneur. She runs different online businesses that are related to clothing and fitness and leaves a lavish lifestyle which is evident from her social media devices.

Lucy Mecklenburgh Bio & Family

The Entrepreneur was born on 24 August 1991, in Havering London, the United Kingdom. Her family includes her father, Paul Mecklenburgh and Linda Mecklenburgh accompanied by her two sisters named Christie Mecklenburgh and Lydia Mecklenburgh being the youngest.

Speaking of her fitness regime, Lucy utilizes most of her free times in proper workout routines and healthy diet. Additionally, she loves to connect with her social media admirers from her fitness YouTube channel titled Results with Lucy; she started back on 17 May 2013.

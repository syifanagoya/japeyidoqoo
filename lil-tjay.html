Lil Tjay (born April 30, 2001) is an American Musical Artist, Rapper, Singer, Songwriter, and Social Media Star from South Bronx, New York. He first gained immense popularity after the release of his song “Pop Out”.

It was released on February 1, 2019, in collaboration with Polo G (American rapper and singer). Pop Out’s music video came out on January 13, 2019, on Tjay’s official self-entitled YouTube channel. It received over 140 million views on YouTube.

Later, Columbia Records distributed the song. Furthermore, the hit track also got the number 11 position on the US Billboard Hot 100. In fact, Billboard magazine ranked it 35th on their 100 Best Songs of 2019 list.

Discography, Rise to Fame & Net worth

In 2017, the rapper started to upload his music on SoundCloud. His songs such as Resume and Brothers received a lot of appreciation. Later, Tjay participated in the Coast 2 Coast LIVE NYC All Ages Edition competition.

In fact, he even bagged placed the first position in the event held on March 10, 2018. Moreover, this competition got him the opportunity to sign a record deal with Columbia Records.

Lil Tjay

The 18-years-old launched his YouTube channel on January 27, 2018. He uploaded his first video titled “Lil TJAY – Resume (Official Music Video)” which received more than 17 million views as of February 2020.

Later, the rapper released his various songs on the platform such as “Ride for You”, “Brothers”, “New Flex ft. Asian Doll”, “Forever”, “Tear us Apart”, etc. In July 2018, the rapper worked with producer Cash Money AP in order to release “None of Your Love”.

The song received more than 18 million views within a few months. In addition, the producer also made various songs for him such as YoungBoy Never Broke Again, Lil Skies, and Ty Dolla Sign.

He then released his debut Extended Play “No Comparison” at the end of 2018. Its song song “Ready for War” was the most successful. Last year, on October 11, 2019, the artist published his debut studio album “True 2 Myself”.

The album featured big artists such as Lil Baby and Lil Wayne. The accumulated Net worth of Lil Tjay is $3 million US Dollars, as of 2020.

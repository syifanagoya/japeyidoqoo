Love Island: Which couples are still together? Ekin-Su and Davide, Andrew and Tasha and MORE

July 04, 2024
blog

Although the point of Love Island is to spend the summer in a villa, meet beautiful people and fall in love, in reality most of our favourite islander couples don't tend to last the test of time, no matter how much we want them to.

However, there is the occasional couple who break the villa curse and actually stay together! Check out the Love Island contestants who haven't had their heads turned and are still coupled up to this day…

 Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu and Davide Sanclimenti (2022 series winners)

The most recent Love Island champions are Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu and Davide Sanclimenti who were crown winners in the summer 2022 series. The couple had quite the journey when they were in the Majorcan villa (who could forget Davide's line 'You're a liar, an actress!' plus Ekin-Su's iconic terrace crawl?) and even called it quits a couple of times while in there. 

Upon leaving the villa, Ekin-Su and Davide broke up once more in July 2023, however they've since reconciled. 

Molly-Mae Hague and Tommy Fury (2019 series)

Molly-Mae and Tommy are arguably two of the most successful contestants to come from the ITV series, and we're hardly surprised to hear that this power couple are still together. The couple moved in together not long after the show finished airing and now are proud parents to a daughter named Bambi. They announced their engagement in July.  

Tasha Ghouri and Andrew la Page (2022 series)

2022 finalists Andrew La Page and Tasha Ghouri are another couple from last year's series who had their fair share of ups and downs, but they seem to still be the picture of romantic bliss. The pair are often seen on each other's social media enjoying cute date nights and days out together. Andrew even recently called Tasha his soulmate – aw! 

Indiyah Polack and Dami Hope (2022 series)

Fellow finalists Indiyah Polak and Dami Hope are certainly a fan favourite couple from the ITV dating show. The pair got together while in the Majorcan villa and, apart from a brief break-up after Casa Amor, they have gone from strength to strength. Dami and Indiyah have even gone a step further and moved in together! 

Nathan Massey and Cara De La Hoyde (2016 series)

Nathan and Cara won the 2016 series, and the pair are still going strong to this day! After getting engaged in 2018, the couple tied the knot in Kent a year later. Prior to their stunning wedding ceremony at Preston Court Barn in Canterbury, the Love Island winners had already welcomed a son, Freddie-George in December 2017. They eventually added to their adorable brood, with daughter Delilah born in July 2020. 

 

Alex Bowen and Olivia Buckland (2016 series)

They might not have won, but they certainly won each other's hearts! Olivia and Alex fell head over heels after meeting in the villa in 2016 and tied the knot back in 2018. Fast forward to 2023, and the couple have now welcomed their first child together. In June 2022, Olivia shared the first photo of their new bundle of joy with the family posing together in an Instagram post. "You are everything. 10/06/22," she captioned the photo showing the baby boy that they have named their baby boy Abel Jacob Bowen.

Jessica Shears and Dom Lever (2017 series)

Jessica and Dom met in the villa back in 2017 and have since married and welcomed children together. They tied the knot in 2018 during a small ceremony in Mykonos. Their first child, a boy named Presley, was born in 2019 and they became a family of four with the arrival of a second baby boy in 2022.

Camilla Thurlow and Jamie Jewitt (2017 series)

This one warms our hearts! After struggling to connect with anyone in the villa, former explosive ordnance disposal specialist Camilla finally met her Prince Charming when Jamie walked through the door. Six years after leaving the villa together, the pair have not only gotten hitched but welcomed their first child together, a daughter named Nell. They then tied the knot in 2021 and announced in 2022 that they had become proud parents to another daughter named Nora.

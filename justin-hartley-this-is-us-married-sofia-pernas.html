Congrats to Justin and Sofia!

    T star Justin Hartley is married!

    The 44-year-old has wed his former co-star Sofia Pernas, less than a year after they were rumored to have started dating.

    "I'm really happy with where I am," he said. "I'm happy with my career. I'm happy with my personal life. I'm healthy, I'm safe."
